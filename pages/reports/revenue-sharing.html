Agreement Notes

This is a 5-year revenue sharing agreement between the Ankole Cultural Cow Conservation Association (ACCCA) and the Uganda Wildlife Authority (UWA). The agreement covers all tourism activities conducted on shared lands and requires a 20% revenue share to be paid to UWA on a quarterly basis.
